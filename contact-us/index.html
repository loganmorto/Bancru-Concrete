Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Bancru Concrete? ▼

    Bancru Concrete Services LLC is a trusted subcontractor specializing in high-quality concrete work for general contractors and homeowners in North Carolina.

  • What services do you offer? ▼

    We provide a wide range of concrete services, including foundations, slabs, sidewalks, driveways, decorative concrete, grading, and erosion control.

  • Are you licensed and insured? ▼

    Yes, we carry liability insurance and workers' compensation. We operate as a subcontractor for general contractors and do not hold a general contractor’s license.

  • Do you work with homeowners? ▼

    Yes, while our primary focus is commercial projects, we also take on residential projects such as driveways, patios, and home foundations.

  • How do I get a quote? ▼

    You can request a quote by emailing us at bancruconcrete@gmail.com. We typically respond within 2-3 business days to schedule a site visit.

  • What areas do you serve? ▼

    We serve the Central Piedmont region of North Carolina, including the Triangle, Triad, Alamance, Charlotte, Asheville, and surrounding areas.

  • What is the process for working with you? ▼

    All projects start with a site visit. After the initial consultation we will provide a quote, and, once accepted, draft a contract. Work begins with site prep, grading, and concrete pouring.

  • How long does a project typically take? ▼

    It depends on the project. Small residential projects can take a few days, while large commercial projects vary based on scope and scheduling.

  • Do you offer decorative concrete options? ▼

    Yes, we specialize in stamped and stained concrete for patios, walkways, and other outdoor spaces.

  • What sets Bancru Concrete apart from other concrete companies? ▼

    Unlike many concrete companies, we own our own heavy equipment and survey tools, allowing us to handle grading and subgrading in-house, eliminating the need for subcontractors.
